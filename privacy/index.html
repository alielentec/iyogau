Privacy Policy

Effective: April 30, 2026 Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how iYogaU ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you visit iyogau.com or apply for one of our programs. iYogaU is operated by Ali Karimi as a sole proprietorship serving practitioners in Seoul (South Korea), Shanghai (China), and the California Bay Area (United States).

Plain-English summary. We collect the minimum information needed to deliver the program you sign up for: your name, email, payment confirmation, and (only if you choose) your birth details for the Vedic astrology component. We never sell or rent your data. We never use it to advertise or train AI. You can request a copy or deletion at any time by emailing privacy@iyogau.com.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number (optional), country of residence.
  • Application information: short answers about your background, goals, and yoga experience submitted through the application form.
  • Astrology information (optional): birth date, birth time, and birth location, only if you choose to enroll in the natal chart component.
  • Health self-disclosure (during enrollment): any medical conditions or pregnancy status you choose to disclose so we can adapt the practice for safety.
  • Payment confirmation: we receive a transaction reference from our payment processor. We do not store your full card number, bank details, or other payment credentials.
  • Course interaction: journal entries you submit through the iYogaU platform during your program.
  • Communications: the content of emails or messages you send us.

Information collected automatically

  • Server access logs: our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) logs IP addresses and request metadata for fraud and abuse prevention. We do not run our own analytics.
  • Browser-level information: your browser sends standard HTTP headers (browser type, language preference, referring page) to load the site.

iYogaU does not deploy Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, advertising trackers, session replay tools, or other behavioral tracking on this website.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiry and process your application.
  • To deliver the program: schedule sessions, review journal entries, prepare the Vedic chart analysis, send course materials.
  • To process payment and issue receipts.
  • To send important program-related communications (schedule changes, prerequisite reminders).
  • To send our newsletter, only if you explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any newsletter email.
  • To comply with legal obligations and to defend our legal rights.

We do not use your information to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models, and we do not allow our service providers to do so with your data.

3. When we share information

We share personal information only in the following limited cases:

  • Payment processor: to charge enrollment fees.
  • Email delivery: a transactional email provider that delivers receipts and program communications.
  • Hosting provider: the static website is hosted on GitHub Pages. The journaling platform is hosted separately under iYogaU control.
  • Legal compliance: if required by valid legal process (subpoena, court order) or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfer: if iYogaU is acquired or merged, your information may transfer to the successor entity, subject to this policy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We do not share information with advertising networks or data brokers.

4. Cookies, fonts, and third-party requests

iYogaU uses the following local browser storage on your device:

  • localStorage entries (iyogau_lang, iyogau_theme) to remember your language and theme preferences. These never leave your browser.
  • sessionStorage entry (iyogau_fx_session) to cache currency exchange rates for the duration of your visit.

We do not set advertising or tracking cookies.

Our website loads Google Fonts from Google's CDN. When fonts load, your browser makes a request to Google, which receives your IP address and standard HTTP headers in the process. Google's handling of that request is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share any other information with Google.

If we add analytics or other non-essential trackers in the future, we will update this policy and present a clear consent notice for visitors in regions that require it (EEA, UK, California, etc.) before any non-essential tracker runs.

5. How long we keep information

  • Application and enrollment records: kept for the duration of your relationship with iYogaU plus seven (7) years for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Journal entries: retained as long as your iYogaU platform account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: kept until you unsubscribe.
  • Email correspondence: kept for up to three (3) years from the last interaction unless required longer for legal reasons.

6. How we protect information

We use TLS (HTTPS) for all transmissions of personal information. The journaling platform is access-controlled and protected with reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

7. California rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, gives you the following rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we have collected, used, disclosed, or sold about you in the past 12 months.
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. iYogaU does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@iyogau.com from the email address associated with your account. We will respond within 45 days as required by law. You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf; we will require written authorization.

Categories of personal information we collected in the past 12 months: identifiers (name, email), customer records (application content), commercial information (enrollment), internet activity (server logs), inferences (program suitability, derived from your application), and — only if you opted in — sensitive astrological birth details.

Categories of sources: directly from you, and indirectly from your device when you visit our site.

Categories of recipients: payment processor, email provider, hosting provider, and legal authorities when required.

We do not knowingly sell or share personal information about consumers under 16 years of age.

8. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

iYogaU does not sell your personal information for money. iYogaU does not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

If California law's definition of "sale" or "sharing" ever applies to any future feature, you can opt out by:

  • Emailing privacy@iyogau.com with the subject "Do Not Sell or Share."
  • Sending a Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal from your browser. We honor GPC as a valid opt-out request.

9. EEA / UK rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the GDPR (and UK GDPR):

  • Right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Our legal bases for processing: (a) performance of a contract (program delivery), (b) legitimate interests (responding to inquiries, preventing fraud), (c) consent (newsletter, optional astrology data), and (d) legal obligation (tax records).

When personal information is transferred outside the EEA/UK, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

10. Korea (PIPA) and China (PIPL) rights

If you are in South Korea, the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) gives you the right to access, correct, delete, and request suspension of processing of your personal information held by us. If you are in mainland China, the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) provides similar rights along with the right to refuse automated decision-making. Contact privacy@iyogau.com to exercise any of these rights. We will respond as required by applicable law.

11. Children's privacy

iYogaU programs are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. For material changes, we will provide reasonable notice (e.g., a banner on the website or an email to active practitioners) before the changes take effect.
